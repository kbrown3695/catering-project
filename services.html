Egerton University Catering Department

Our Catering Services

We provide a range of catering services designed to meet the needs of students, staff, and event organizers at Egerton University. We have diverse options tailored for different tastes and preferences:

Mode of Payment

We operate the Pay as You Eat (PAYE) cafeteria system which is a queuing system where you pay before provision of service. Till Mpesa is available in each cafeteria unit.

Catering Department Units

1. Mara- Simba Wing

2. J.F Kennedy Restaurant

3. Mara- Chui Wing

4. Guest Wing restaurant

5. Tsavo Cafeteria

6. Smart Cafeteria

Daily Dining Services

Our dining halls serve a variety of meals each day, including breakfast, lunch, and dinner. We ensure the highest standards of hygiene and quality in our meals.

Special Event Catering

We cater for events such as university meetings, workshops, and student events. Our catering packages can be customized to meet specific needs and budgets.

Specialized Diet Options

We understand that some people have dietary restrictions. We offer specialized menus that include vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options.
